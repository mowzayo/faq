FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges. Free challenges give you access to various projects, but premium challenges unlock more complex projects and extra resources.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! All projects on Frontend Mentor are meant to help you build a portfolio with real-world examples.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can ask for help in the community forum, where other developers share their experiences and help each other out.
